C’est quoi Payote ?

A French brand story shaped by sun, craft, and movement.

Born between the Mediterranean and the Pyrenees, Payote blends southern ease, French espadrille heritage, and a more conscious way of making. This page is the gateway to the brand: where it began, how it produces, what it rethinks, and what it chooses to stand for.

Why this page matters

It gives the brand a voice before it asks for the sale.

A strong brand story page is not decorative. It reassures, differentiates, and gives meaning to the products. For an international audience, it is often the place where trust begins: not with pricing, but with origin, clarity, and coherence.

That is why this version sits above the subpages. “Our Story” becomes the entry point, while “Nos premiers pas”, “Made in France”, “Recyclage”, and “Nos actions” become structured deeper reads.
